Iron Skin Potion

For Mortals

A delightfully healthy smoothie packed full of berries for a delicious flavour, combined with oats to make the smoothie extra filling making it easy to get through the day without feeling hungry again.

For Sorcerers

A rare potion known for its abilities to strengthen the drinker's skin making it practically impenetrable. It was often used in the past before warriors would go into battle but in more recent years by professional fighters.

How to make it

Ingredients

  • 450g bag frozen berry
  • 450g pot fat-free strawberry yoghurt
  • 100ml milk
  • 25g porridge oat
  • 2 tsp honey

Instructions

Combine your ingredients together within the blender and blend until smooth and frothy. Optionally mix through oats if you want your smoothie to be extra filling

Serve into a glass and enjoy! Optionally serve with a drizzle of honey if you prefer your smoothie extra sweet.
